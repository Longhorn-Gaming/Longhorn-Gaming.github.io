Smash Ultimate

The Longhorn Smash Ultimate team started in Fall 2017 and proceeded to get dead last at our first event. After this we made several changes to our infrastructure including monthly practice and review sessions. At our next event in Spring 2019 we placed 3rd overall. Following that, we won our very first event in the Summer of 2019. Since then, we've consistently placed in the top 2 at every event since then. Our most notable finish was tying for first at the 2020 CSL Regionals. Our goal for the 2019-2020 season was to finish top 4 in the nation. Unfortunately our season was cut short due to COVID, but our goal remains the same for 2021.
